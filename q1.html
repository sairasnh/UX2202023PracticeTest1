Explanation

Using <em> and <strong> tags for emphasizing and strengthening text is preferred over and tags because they provide semantic meaning to the content. <em> indicates emphasis, while <strong> indicates strong importance. This semantic markup makes it easier for assistive technologies, search engines, and CSS styles to interpret and style the content correctly. On the other hand, and tags are more presentation-oriented and do not convey the same level of meaning, making the content less accessible and harder to style consistently.
